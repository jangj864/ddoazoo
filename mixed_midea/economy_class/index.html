Economy Class (2024)

'ECONOMY CLASS' is a project that showcases the resilience of artists in the face of challenges brought about by the COVID-19 pandemic. Despite the obstacles, artists worldwide - including Korea, Japan, the United States, the Philippines, and Canada - found ways to collaborate and create, resulting in a diverse and dynamic mixtape.

Overall, DDOAZOO's 'ECONOMY CLASS' is a collaborative project highlighting artists' creativity and resilience in challenging times. This is the first mixtape in the Flight trilogy, with plans to produce the following projects, Business Class and First Class, later.
