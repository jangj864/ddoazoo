Ai Melody

AI Melody is a pioneering startup that provides an innovative web-based platform and applications that enable customers to make detailed music production requests. Our key products include a cutting-edge composition platform and AI-to-speech VST plugins designed to deliver exceptional musical compositions to suit the needs of both clients and composers. As a designer at AI Melody, I laid the foundation for the initial branding and design.

My role involved designing an intuitive and visually appealing user interface for the platform, ensuring a smooth and engaging user experience. I collaborated closely with our development team to integrate the AI speech conversion and data generation tools that are core to our product.

I played a crucial role in establishing AI Melody's identity as a leader in the AI-based music creation space by implementing solid design principles. This project not only helped me hone my UI/UX design skills but also allowed me to explore innovative solutions that protect intellectual property and ensure the safe and ethical use of voice data.
