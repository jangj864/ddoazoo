Mars Spaceline (2023)

With the continuous development of the aerospace industry, the dream of space travel will soon come true. After the invention of airplanes, airlines, and travel agencies appeared in various parts of the world. Soon, there will be a space agency to go to Mars.

These are designs and illustrations of the spaceline's logo, spacecraft, spacecraft, tickets, stickers, and bags. These are materials that people will likely use in the future. Designing a spaceship of a distant future would be too unrealistic or remote, so I redesigned them by modifying the current satellites. On the luggage, there are stickers with some cute characters.

Description

Mars Property Exchange (2023)

I designed the UX/UI of an astronaut helmet for Mars traveling. The various functional sensors are mounted on the helmet, and the interface information is displayed. And the user will experience more convenient space exploration. The Mars-only unique information includes the gravity and Standard Time on Mars.

In addition, the helmet provides the Mars Property Exchange System in AR. As Mars develops, people will try to buy land on Mars. People can invest in and trade virtual real estate on Mars from this system.
