Fall 2023 Core 1 Typography's
Project 6: Wiki Book

This project was to select one of the Wikipedia articles and create a book about it, and this book I created contains content about hamburgers. In addition to the contents of the book, the cover, edges, dust jacket, and even the box containing the book were packaged to make the book more than just a means of conveying information, but also interactive in design.

Book

Iframe E-Book

Overall, elements, colors, and fonts related to hamburgers and fast food restaurants were used.

Cover

Size : 11 x 11 inches

The cover of the book is decorated with sesame seeds so that you can tell who is responsible for the hamburger even if the title of the book is not written separately, and the ingredients are placed on the edge next to the book.

Dust Jacket

The dust jacket surrounding the hamburger-shaped book was also designed in the shape of hamburger wrapping paper.

Package & Box Grid

Book Package & Box Grid

The package containing the book was also designed in the form of a large burger box.
