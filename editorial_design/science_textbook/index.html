SCIENCE TEXTBOOK COVER (2020)​

Award at 26th (2020) International Communication Design Competition (Mirae-N Textbook cover design contest)/Communication Design Association of Korea. I designed a Science textbook targeting the higher elementary school grades.

The cover was designed with the written word ‘Science’ in Korean. In addition, I illustrated various scientific materials to express typography. For children who think these subjects are complex. Those designed supplies express them more friendly.

Science Textbook Cover

Science Textbook Cover

By creating typography for each character of the word '과학 (Science)' using scientific instruments, I made the subject of science more accessible and friendly to the elementary school audience.
