This is the Page off the magazine that I worked on in the 'Editorial Design' course. The project was designed through a project to create page spreads by selecting one of my favorite artists. I decided to introduce my favorite Brit pop band Oasis. Their iconic item, the Gibson guitar is placed in the middle of the page. The logos they used are placed on each part of the guitar's neck. To demonstrate the mood of the 90s, I expressed the texture of the wrinkled paper with faded and torn ends.

Page

Spread #1

In the first spread, a guitar symbolizing the band is placed, and the history of the logo is included in each fingerboard part. Also, to give a feeling of the 90s, faded black and white colors were used.

The next page is an extension of the previous page and is a little simpler, but shares a similar feeling.
