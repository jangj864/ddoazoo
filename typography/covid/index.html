Spring 2020 Typography's
Korean Typography Project

This is a typography created in the 2020 'Typography 2' course.

The concept was produced under the theme of COVID-19 when the pandemic was declared. This is a Korean font for titles with the main focus on covid. The letters indicate "Covid virus - a respiratory syndrome caused by infection."

COVID-19 has been the most significant issue in past years. I incorporated different virus shapes into the typeface and made them into various forms. I intuitively expressed the form of the virus so that the most prominent feature of this font is the virus.

In addition to placing the shapes in several suitable locations, I showed the danger of covid by using a rapidly broken, irregular shape from the irregularity of the material
