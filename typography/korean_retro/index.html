Korean Retro Style Advertisement Poster​​​​​​ (2019)​

The advertising poster for the Korean figure company is a nostalgic trip down memory lane. The poster showcases a range of intricately designed figures that capture the essence of Korean retro culture. The poster features a selection of old words that were commonly used in Korea back in the day.

Each word has been illustrated with great attention to detail, using colors and fonts reminiscent of the era. These words add to the poster's charm and serve as a testament to the enduring legacy of Korean culture.

Chinese characters have also been incorporated into the design to enhance the nostalgic vibe further.

The combination of Korean words, Chinese characters, and the unique design of the figures creates a visually striking poster that transports viewers back in time.

Whether you are a fan of Korean culture or simply appreciate vintage aesthetics, this poster will surely capture your imagination and leave a lasting impression.
