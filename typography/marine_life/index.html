Marine Life Typeface

Expanding our thinking on environmental protection, I wanted to shed light on the often overlooked and invisible world of marine life. I aimed to the notion that what we cannot see is unimportant. By focusing on the often-overlooked marine life in the deep sea, I sought to raise awareness about the interdependence of all life forms on our planet, even those that are not readily visible to us. This is the font that I made inspired by marine life.

This is a marine life font that captures the morphological features of various aquatic creatures, using patterns, shapes, and curves to create a soft, flowing aesthetic that evokes the feeling of being underwater. Throughout this foundation, I wanted to capture the beauty and diversity of marine life and inspire people to take action to protect it.
